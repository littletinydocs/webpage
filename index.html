About

Little Tiny Docs is about the creation of original documentaries that last up to 5 minutes, excluding initial and end credits, centered on the exploration of a space understood as a place of intersections.

The selected space should be approached as a territory where different dimensions of social life intersect. Namely people, trajectories, times, memories, uses of the place, daily rhythms and social dynamics.

The objective is not only to record a space, but to understand and reveal the relationships that are established within it.

Theme

Space as a Place of Intersections

The film should avoid a merely descriptive approach, seeking instead to construct an audiovisual reading that highlights the complexity of space as a lived and shared reality.

In this sense, space should not be treated as a setting, but as a central element of the documentary narrative.

The proposal should demonstrate conceptual clarity, coherence between form and content, and mastery of audiovisual media, namely in terms of image, sound, and editing.

Submissions

Eligibility

Short documentary films and experimental audiovisual works from any country are accepted.

Format
